#5 - NOODLE WITH MINCED PORK BEAN SAUCE- 炸醬麵

This is a favorite lunch in my household. I make it almost every week. When we sit down to eat, each of us will has our own personal hot sauce with different flavor and degree of spiciness.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 cucumber
  • 200g dry noodle
  • 200g minced pork
  • 1-Cup chopped vegetable (onion, carrot, celery string beans etc.)
  • 1 TBSP cooking wine
  • 1 TBSP potato starch (or corn starch) mix with ¼ Cup water for thickening

SAUCE:

  • 2TBSP dark soy sauce
  • 1 ½ TBSP bean paste
  • 1 TBSP hoi sin sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 180ml water

Directions:

  1. Cook the noodle per package instruction.
  2. Cut the cucumber into fine julienne. Set aside.
  3. Heat up 2 TBSP oil in frying pan, put in the minced pork.
  4. Sauté the minced pork for a few min, add cooking wine. Remove and set aside.
  5. Add a bit more oil in the frying pan, put in the chopped vegetable and sauté for a few minutes.
  6. Add in the meat, and all the ingredients for the sauce.
  7. Bring to boil, and then let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  8. Add the potato starch mixture while stirring to thicken the sauce.
  9. Put noodle in a bowl, top with minced pork and cucumber.
  10. Serve with personal favorite hot sauce.

(Serves 1 person)
