#14 - ZUCCHINI PORK PIE - 翠玉瓜肉末扣餅

This the Chinese version of the stuffed pizza pie.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 cup (120g) All-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup (60ml) very warm (60ºC) water
  • ¼ tsp Salt
  • 120 g Minced pork
  • 1 (80g) zucchini
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Minced pork seasoning:

  • 1 tsp Soy sauce
  • 1 tsp cooking wine
  • ¼ tsp Sugar
  • ½ tsp Salt
  • ½ TBSP Potato starch
  • 1 tsp Sesame oil
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • pepper

Directions:

  1. Put the flour and ¼ tsp salt in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Add the water. Mix and knead the dough to form a ball. Rest for 15 minutes.
  3. Mix the minced pork with all the seasonings.
  4. Using chopsticks, stir the minced meat vigorously in circular motion until it becomes sticky pasty consistence.
  5. Wash and shred the zucchini with a shredder.
  6. Put the shredded zucchini in a muslin cloth and squeeze some excessive water from the zucchini.
  7. Add the zucchini into the minced pork and mix well. Roughly, divide into 10 equal portions by drawing lines in the meat stuffing.
  8. Divide the dough into 10 equal pieces.
  9. Using a rolling pin, roll each portion into a 4-inch circle.
  10. Put one portion of the meat stuffing onto a piece of the dough. Spread the stuffing evenly, leaving about ½ inch edge around.
  11. Put another piece of the dough on top. Press the middle gently to remove air pocket. Press the edge firmly.
  12. Using the edge of a bowl, press hardly along the circumference of the pie, cutting off ¼ inch of the excessive dough from the outer edge of the dough and form a nice round pie.
  13. Repeat steps 10 – 12 for the rest of the dough and meat stuffing.
  14. Heat a frying pan (preferably cast iron) with 2 TBSP oil. When the oil is hot, line the pie in a single layer on the frying pan without touching each other.
  15. With medium high heat, cook until the bottom side of the pie to light brown.
  16. Turn the pies over, add ½ cup of water in the pan and immediately cover.
  17. Let it cook until the water in the frying pan almost evaporated.
  18. Open the lid and let the moisture further evaporate and both sides golden brown.
  19. Remove and serve hot.

(Serves 1 person)

Note:
  • Serve with your favorite hot sauce, Zhenjiang vinegar鎮江醋, or Worcestershire sauce.
