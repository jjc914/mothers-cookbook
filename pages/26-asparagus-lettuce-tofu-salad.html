#26 - ASPARAGUS LETTUCE AND TOFU SALAD - 窩筍拌素雞

Asparagus lettuce is the stem of a special variety of lettuce. The stem is about 1 ½ - 2 inches in diameter and in the shape of an asparagus. Vegetarian Chicken 素雞is a seasoned firm tofu with its texture and color resembles chicken meat.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 Asparagus lettuce
  • 2 vegetarian chicken 素雞
  • 1 green onion chopped
  • 1 cilantro chopped
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 TBSP vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • A few drops sesame oil

Directions:

  1. Peel of the skin of asparagus lettuce using a vegetable peeler. Remove all whitish hard fibers running along the stem.
  2. Rinse in water and slice into 2-inch-long julienne slices.
  3. Put in in a bowl and add ½ tsp of salt. Mix well and let it rest in the refrigerator for 2 hours.
  4. Steam the vegetarian chicken for 15 min and let cool completely.
  5. Cut it into similar size as the asparagus lettuce. Set aside.
  6. Take out the asparagus lettuce. Put it in a cheesecloth and squeeze out some excessive water.
  7. Put it in a plate with chopped green onion on top.
  8. Heat up 2 TBSP vegetable oil. Pour the oil onto the asparagus lettuce with the green onion on top. It should sizzle.
  9. Mix well.
  10. Add in the vegetarian chicken, then the sugar and sesame oil. Toss gently.
  11. Top with chopped cilantro.

(Serves 1 person)

Note:
  • The different names for asparagus lettuce are: stem lettuce, celery lettuce, and celtuce.
