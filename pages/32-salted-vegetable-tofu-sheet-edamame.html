#32 - SALTED VEGETABLE WITH TOFU SHEET & EDEMAME - 鹹菜百頁毛豆

This is a vegetarian dish which you can find in many Shanghainese restaurants.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 6 sheet (approx. 175g) thick tofu sheet (百頁)
  • 200g fresh/frozen edamame with skin on
  • 200g shanghai vegetable (小棠菜)
  • 1 TBSP coarse salt
  • ¼ red bell pepper, sliced thin strip
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • 2 slices ginger
  • 2 shallots, diced
  • 1 cup Chicken / vegetable broth
  • 1 tsp sugar

Directions:

  1. Wash and dice Shanghai vegetable. Put it in a bowl and mix in the coarse salt.
  2. Pack the salted vegetable in a jar and put it in the refrigerator for at least 1 day.
  3. Take out the salted vegetable and wash it in fresh water. Squeeze dry and set aside.
  4. Boil edamame in water for 3 min, drain. Remove the shell and set aside.
  5. Slice tofu sheet into 3 x ¼ - inches strip.
  6. Soak in hot water with baking soda for 1/2 hr. Drain and rinse.
  7. In a wok with hot oil, put in diced shallot, then the tofu sheet. Stir-fry for a few min then add in water/broth. Cook until the tofu sheet is tender and the broth reduced to ½ cup. Remove and set aside.
  8. Heat in a wok again; add oil, then the ginger. Cook until fragrant. Add the salted vegetable and stir-fry. Add a bit water if necessary to cook the vegetable until soft.
  9. Add in the edamame, the tofu sheet and the red bell pepper. Mix well.
  10. Add 1 tsp sugar and taste.
  11. Put on serving plate.

(Serves 1 person)

Note:
  • The dish should not be dry. There should be some sauce left in it when serve.
