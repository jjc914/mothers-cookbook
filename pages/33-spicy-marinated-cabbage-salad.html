#33 - SPICY MARINATED CABBAGE SALAD - 糖醋洋白菜

This is the spicy version of the marinated cucumber dish. I like to make either one of them for my dinner parties. It is refreshing and appetizing.

INGREDIENTS:

  • ½ head cabbage (300g)
  • 1 TBSP oil
  • 1 tsp Sichuan peppercorn
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 red bell pepper
  • 2-3 dry red pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 TBSP sugar
  • 3 TBSP vinegar

Directions:

  1. Wash and cut cabbage to bite size.
  2. Marinate cabbage in salt for at least 2 hours, and then drain excess water.
  3. While the cabbage is marinating, peel and cut garlic into pieces. Cut the dry red pepper into pieces also.
  4. Wash and deseed red bell pepper and slice into long strips like the size of the cabbage.
  5. Heat oil in a small saucepan until hot. Add peppercorn and dry red pepper into oil.
  6. Stir and cook until it is aromatic, and then turn off heat. Wait and let the oil cool.
  7. Meanwhile, mix sugar and vinegar together.
  8. When the oil cools off a bit, add the sugar and vinegar mixture into the oil. Mix well.
  9. Add the mixture into the drained cabbage.
  10. Add the rest of the ingredients (garlic and red bell pepper).
  11. Put everything into a clean glass jar. Pack it tight.
  12. Let it marinate for at least 4 hours (better overnight).
  13. Turn the jar if necessary to let the cabbage touch the sauce.
  14. Pick out the excess peppercorn before serve.

(Serves 1 person)

Note:
  • This dish can be made a few days before serve.
