#37 - NEW YEAR CAKE (NIANGAO) - 糖年糕

This the most popular Chinese New Year food. You can find it everywhere before the Chinese New Year. This homemade version uses only a few ingredients and is not that hard to make.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 500 g glutinous rice flour
  • 100 g wheat starch
  • 400 g slab sugar (片糖)
  • 600 ml (1 ¼ cup) water
  • 3 TBSP vegetable oil
  • ½ piece red date for garnish
  • 1-2 eggs
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Directions:

  1. Put the slab sugar and water in a saucepan. Bring to boil and simmer until all the sugar is dissolved.
  2. Let the sugar water cool to 60ºC
  3. Mix the glutinous rice flour and wheat starch and put it through a sieve into a big bowl.
  4. Pour the sugar water into the flour mixture slowly, stirring with a wooden spatula continually, trying to break up the lumps.
  5. Mix in vegetable oil.
  6. Pass the mixture through a sieve to make sure that there is no lumps in it.
  7. Oil an approx. 1200 ml circular container.
  8. Pour the mixture into a circular container, gently knock the container on the counter to make remove air bubbles and smooth out top surface.
  9. Garnish with a pitted red date.
  10. Heat a steamer big enough to hold the circular container.
  11. When the water boils, put in the rice cake mixture and steam for 1 ½ hour in low heat.
  12. Let the rice cake cool completely and then put in the refrigerator for at least 24 hours to harden.
  13. Before serving, remove the rice cake from the refrigerator, slice it into 1 ½ x 2 x ½ inch slices.
  14. Beat the eggs and dip the rice cake into the egg mixture.
  15. Heat up a frying pan with oil.
  16. When oil is hot, line the dipped rice cake in the frying pan, single layer.
  17. Fry on low heat, until both side of the rice cake is lightly brown.
  18. Serve.

(Serves 1 person)

Note:
  • If you cannot wheat starch, you may swap it out by equal amount of glutinous rice flour. The finished product will be a bit stickier and chewier.
  • Slab sugar is raw cane sugar in the shape of rectangular slab. You may use other kinds of raw cane sugar if you cannot find slab sugar.
  • It is important to let the sugar water to cool down before mixing with the flour, so it will not cook the flour and make it very hard to stir and lumpy.
  • After step 11, the rice cake is ready to eat. However, it is very gluey and sticky, and different to cut. I prefer to serve it after it hardens and re-fried.
