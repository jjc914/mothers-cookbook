#39 - COCONUT OSMANTHUS NEW YEAR CAKE - 椰汁桂花年糕

INGREDIENTS:

  • 600 g glutinous rice flour
  • 3 TBSP wheat starch
  • 450g rock sugar (冰糖)
  • 200 ml water
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 3 TBSP vegetable oil
  • 1tsp dry osmanthus flower

Directions:

  1. Put the rock sugar and water in a saucepan. Bring to boil and simmer until all the sugar is dissolved.
  2. Mix in the coconut milk.
  3. Mix the glutinous rice flour and the wheat starch in a big mixing bowl.
  4. Add the sugar and coconut solution slowly into the flour while stirring.
  5. Mix in vegetable oil.
  6. Pass the mixture through a sieve to make sure that there is no lumps in it.
  7. Oil an approx. 1200 ml circular container.
  8. Pour the mixture into a circular container, gently knock the container on the counter to make remove air bubbles and smooth out top surface.
  9. Sprinkle the Osmanthus flower on top.
  10. Heat a steamer big enough to hold the circular container.
  11. When the water boils, put in the rice cake mixture and steam for 1 ½ hour in low heat.
  12. Let the rice cake cool completely and then put in the refrigerator for at least 24 hours to harden.
  13. Before serving, remove the rice cake from the refrigerator, slice it into 1 ½ x 2 x ½ inch slices.
  14. Fry on low heat, until both side of the rice cake is lightly brown.
  15. Serve.
